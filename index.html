City University's vision, as stated on its website, is to become a leading institution of higher learning, creating a culture of excellence and accelerating national development by producing high-quality human resources for the 21st century and beyond

City University

City University is a private university in Dhaka, Bangladesh. It was established in 2002. The language of instruction of City University is English

Promoting Accessibility

City University provides more than an academic education, but a holistic approach to higher education including competitive athletics to social activities

Flexibility and Inclusion

City University offers a wide variety of programs and courses to fit your needs. From full-time bachelor programs to evening masters programs over a span of two campus locations

Program Offerings

Available programs can be found in the respective department page with further details on course offerings and credit requirements

University Life
